Project Overview

About the Renovation

The Diamond Jubilee Hall at the College of Engineering Trivandrum has been the heart of campus events for decades — hosting CETAA Days, convocations, cultural programmes, and distinguished lectures. The proposed renovation will modernise this cherished landmark while honouring its legacy.

Central Air Conditioning

Full HVAC system to make the hall comfortable year-round for events, seminars, and gatherings of up to 500 attendees.

Modern Seating

Replacement of existing seating with ergonomic, theatre-style chairs that offer improved visibility and comfort throughout the hall.

AV & Stage Upgrades

Professional audio-visual systems, LED projection, and stage lighting installations for seminars, cultural performances, and alumni events.

Facade & Surroundings

Exterior painting, landscaping, parking, and facade improvements to present a dignified and welcoming entrance worthy of CET's reputation.
