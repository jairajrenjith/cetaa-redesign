Our Story

Welcome to CET Alumni

The College of Engineering, Trivandrum was established in 1939 as the first Engineering College in the then Travancore State. The first classes were started on 3rd July 1939 during the reign of the Travancore King, Sri Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma. His vision for technical education planted the seed of what would become one of India's most respected engineering institutions.

Initially housed in the former office and bungalow of the Chief Engineer (present PMG Office), CET has grown into a sprawling campus of intellectual excellence. The College of Engineering Trivandrum Alumni Association (CETAA) now proudly connects thousands of alumni across the globe — a living testament to CET's enduring legacy.

CETAA facilitates professional networking, mentorship, scholarships, campus development, and cultural events that keep the CET spirit alive in every alumnus, wherever they are in the world.
